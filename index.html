About Me

I am a Scientific Research Officer with specialization in Educational Development and Political Science. My research interests include American foreign policy, summit diplomacy, foreign policy decision-making, and teaching methodology in International Relations. With a background in both research and teaching, I focus on enhancing academic teaching practices and internationalization through various European projects.
